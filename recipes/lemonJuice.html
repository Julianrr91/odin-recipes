Lasagna

Description

This is a tradittional italian lasagna recipe same as the one nona use to make

Ingridients

  • Tomatoes
  • Beef meat
  • Onions
  • Sheet pasta

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with watter
  2. Set the grill to medium heat
  3. Cut the tomatoes in halfs and put it in the watter
  4. Set up a pan and put it in low heat
  5. Add a bit of oil to the pan and put the beef meat
